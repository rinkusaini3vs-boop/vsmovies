Dhurandhar is a blockbuster Indian spy thriller directed by Aditya Dhar, starring Ranveer Singh as an Indian spy infiltrating a terrorist network in Karachi's Lyari, Pakistan, featuring a star cast including Akshaye Khanna, Sanjay Dutt, and Arjun Rampal, praised for its technical finesse, gritty realism, and nationalistic themes, sparking debates about propaganda while achieving massive box office success and facing bans in Gulf countries.
